Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 1 baguette, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tomatoes, diced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Toast baguette slices until golden brown.
  2. Mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Top each baguette slice with tomato mixture.
  4. Serve immediately.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and chop them finely.
  3. Mix chopped stems with cream cheese, Parmesan, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper.
  4. Fill mushroom caps with the mixture.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
