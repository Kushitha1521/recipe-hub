Chocolate Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Melt butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and cocoa powder.
  3. Combine flour, salt, and baking powder, then stir into the wet mixture.
  4. Pour batter into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes.

Classic Vanilla Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. In a saucepan, whisk together sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in milk.
  3. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Pour into serving dishes and chill before serving.
